Decision trees are an intuitive way for people to imagine AI working. One might think that it is a series of conditions until we arrive at the proper place. Decision trees tend to be a great first choice in problem solving because they are easy to use and deliver great performance.

Linear regression models are the most useful in the world of ML. These, like decision trees, are also easy to use and very powerful. They use two main variables, a dependant and independant variable. From these two you can use the independant variable to give decent predictsion for the dependant variable.

Neural networks are the most popular model that is used. These are made for more complex problems such as image classification and audio synthetization. They come in small sizes and larger sizes. These models are harder to create and but immensely more powerful than any other model.

KNN stands for K nearest neighbors. These models are really good at picking out patterns that we cannot see ourselves. They follow a type of learning called unsupervised learning and can be great when taking a first look at any given dataset.
